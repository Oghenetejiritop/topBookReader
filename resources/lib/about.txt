	A rich accessible E-book reader with an intuitive interface that is used by book lovers in viewing over five E-book formats such as: DOCX, EPUB, PDF, TXT and so on.
Also includes an offline built-in dictionary to quickly check the meaning of a word. Offers a wide range of Text To Speech (TTS) services to read your book aloud.
